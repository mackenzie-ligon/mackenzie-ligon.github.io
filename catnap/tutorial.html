Catnap Tutorial

Controls:

Press SPACE to jump / Press & Hold to jump higher

Press R to restart

Powerups:

Icecream: slows speed for five seconds (automatically used)

Pie: double jump (can only hold one at a time)

Bugs:

Platform spawning can get Felix stuck. If this happens, wait until prompted, then press U to get unstuck.

I'm ready to play!
